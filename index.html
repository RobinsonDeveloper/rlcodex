How the Partnership Works

Five simple steps from first contact to certified students.

01

MOU Signing

We sign a formal Memorandum of Understanding with your institution. This covers scope, schedule, fee structure and responsibilities for both parties.

Legal AgreementAcademic Year
02

Curriculum Setup

Our team works with college coordinators to align the course syllabus with your university structure, semester schedule and elective framework.

Custom SyllabusUniversity Aligned
03

Student Enrollment

Students register for their chosen technology track. Batches of 25–40 students are formed per track, ensuring quality trainer-to-student ratio.

Batch of 25–40Track Selection
04

Training Delivery

Expert trainers conduct on-campus sessions 3–4 days per week, blending theory, live coding, mini-projects and weekly assessments over 12 months.

On-CampusHybrid OptionLive Projects
05

Certification & Placement

Successful students receive the RL Codexa industry certificate. We assist with placement drives, resume building and mock interview sessions.

RL Codexa CertificatePlacement Support
